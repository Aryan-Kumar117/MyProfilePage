Recipe for Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredient List

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups (aged, long-grain preferred)
  • Chicken - 500-700 g (bone-in pieces work best for flavor)
  • Yogurt (curd) – 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 2–3, slit
  • Red chili powder – 1–2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice – 1 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves – ½ cup, chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves – ½ cup, chopped
  • Salt – to taste

Recipeeeeeee

  1. Marinate chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, green chilies, mint, coriander, lemon juice, salt. Rest 1 hr.
  2. Fry onions: Slice and deep-fry onions till golden (birista). Keep aside.
  3. Parboil rice: Cook basmati rice with whole spices and salt until 70% done. Drain.
  4. Layer: In a heavy pot, spread marinated chicken at the bottom, top with fried onions, then rice. Sprinkle saffron milk, ghee, and a little rose/kewra water.
  5. Dum cook: Seal the pot with dough or tight lid. Cook on high heat for 5 min, then on very low heat for 25–30 min. Rest 10 min before serving.

Cooking time, and Servings

Serves about 4 people, takes 2 hours to marinate, then 1 hour of active cooking.
